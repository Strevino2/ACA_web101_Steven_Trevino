“Any fool can write code that a computer can understand. Good programmers write code that humans can understand.” - Martin Fowler



For more than a third of my life, I have always had a fascination with becoming a developer and I have started this journey this year with Austin Coding Academy. Most people tell me that it is the future when someone asks me about my job and my life but I like to think of this shift in career as a life-style change more than just work-related.


About me:

Born in the hill country of Texas and raised during the 90s, I grew up playing many sports and video games. Good internet was very rare due to the area I lived in but small town living and a great community more than made up for this hindrance. The start of my web development career has its own story filled with fate.

I started my first dive into web development in college at Angelo State University. As with some teenagers at that time, I couldn't make my mind up on what Bachelor degree to settle on. Before I could finish college I was hired with a medical transportation company specializing in medical logistics for the major hospital systems around the San Antonio and Austin area. Quickly worked my way up to Area Manager and excelled at this position. I was eventually laid off due to the pandemic and when our company was bought out by another company from the east coast. I decided then that this was a blessing in disguise.

After scouring the web for a direction after being laid off, I decided to get back on the train to becoming a developer when I ran across the website for Austin Coding Academy. After a few short talks with an admissions adviser and taking a few free weeks of learning courses, my new journey had begun!!


If you would like to find out more about me you can always visit my blog page on this website, browse through some of my work, or get in touch with me through my contact page!
